Logo

The signal-bars mark

A five-bar spectrum cresting in the middle, carrying the green → blue → violet aurora sweep. It pairs with a plain “Skyetrail” wordmark in IBM Plex Sans 600. The mark holds on light and dark; on light backgrounds, use the dark wordmark.

Do

  • Keep the gradient green → blue → violet, left to right.
  • Use the light-wordmark lockup on dark; the dark-wordmark lockup on light.
  • Give the lockup clear space ≈ the height of the mark.
  • Use the favicon tile when the mark must sit below 16px.

Don't

  • Recolor the mark or change the gradient direction.
  • Use the phasor stack as a small logo — it is large-use only.
  • Add a colored “ai” to the wordmark; it is plain “Skyetrail”.
  • Stretch, rotate, or add drop shadows to the lockup.

Motif

The phasor stack

The luminous hero and atmosphere graphic: glowing, phase-offset waveforms stacked in green / blue / violet bands. Rendered live on canvas. Large-use only — it does not survive below ~160px, and is never used as a small logo (that's the signal-bars mark).
